Pros and Cons of PNG Compared to JPG

Pros of PNG

  • Supports transparency, making it ideal for overlays and logos.
  • Retains image quality after editing and saving.
  • Better for text-based images.
  • Supports a wider range of colors.
  • Ideal for high-quality images with no artifacts.

Cons of PNG

  • Produces larger file sizes compared to JPG.
  • Not suited for photographs due to higher file sizes.
  • Slower loading times for web usage due to file size.
  • Limited support for animation compared to GIF.
  • Not compatible with all older systems.
